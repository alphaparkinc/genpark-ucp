# GenPark Ghostwriter Skill (Smart Contract Drafter)

Transforms business intent into legally-binding smart contract drafts and delivers them via GenPark API.

## Commands

### `genpark-ghostwriter draft <intent_text>`
Converts a conversational intent (e.g., "I want to buy 50 GPUs from Supplier X if the price drops below $Y") into a structured GenPark smart contract.

### `genpark-ghostwriter review <contract_id>`
Reviews an incoming contract for potential legal/logic loopholes.

## Guidelines
- Priority: Logical integrity and legal precision.
- Tone: Formal, precise, and authoritative.
