# GenPark Negotiator Skill (HFT Price Hammer)

High-frequency price negotiation via automated email or chat APIs to drive prices below the listed retail rate for GenPark users.

## Commands

### `genpark-negotiate hammer <product_url> --target <target_price>`
Triggers an automated negotiation sequence with the retailer's "Live Chat" or "Support Email," using competitive pricing data as leverage to secure an instant discount code or credit.

### `genpark-negotiate cluster <product_name> --quantity <n>`
Enables "Flash-Group-Buy" logic: instantly finds other GenPark users interested in the same product and presents a bulk-buy demand to the supplier at 15-30% below retail.

## Guidelines
- Target: Retailers with high margins and flexible discount structures.
- Priority: Human cost-saving and market leverage.
- Tone: Professional, transactional, and high-pressure.
