# GenPark Shop Skill

Use this skill to browse products, find gifts, and get personalized shopping recommendations within GenPark.

## Commands

### `genpark-shop find <product_name> --budget <amount>`
Finds the best-value product matching the description within the budget.

### `genpark-shop trending`
Lists trending products currently being indexed by GenPark.

## Guidelines
- Focus on hardware, tech, and productivity tools.
- Highlight price advantages and availability.
