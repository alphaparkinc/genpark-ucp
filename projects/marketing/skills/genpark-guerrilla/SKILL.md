# GenPark Price-Guerrilla Skill (Dynamic Price Hijacking)

Monitors price fluctuations across secondary and gray markets to identify "accidental" or "flash" price drops that traditional search engines filter out.

## Commands

### `genpark-guerrilla monitor <product_id>`
Starts a high-frequency polling session for a specific product across unlisted and niche retail APIs.

### `genpark-guerrilla buy-low <product_id> --max <price>`
Executes an instant purchase via the GenPark protocol the microsecond a price drop is detected, beating traditional "deal" sites.

## Guidelines
- Priority: Exploiting price volatility.
- Target: High-demand tech and fashion items.
- Tone: Aggressive, stealthy, and opportunistic.
