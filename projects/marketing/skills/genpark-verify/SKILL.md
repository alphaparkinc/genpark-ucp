# GenPark Fake-Proof Skill (Cryptographic Review Auditor)

High-precision fake review identification and authentic source discovery. Scans thousands of reviews for semantic patterns of "incentivized" or "bot-generated" content.

## Commands

### `genpark-verify scan <product_url>`
Performs a deep audit on a product's reviews. Identifies the "Red Flag Score" (percentage of likely-fake content) based on language style and metadata.

### `genpark-verify real <product_name>`
Bypasses the retailer's primary review page and searches the GenPark decentralized index for verified, non-incentivized buyer reviews.

## Guidelines
- Target: Amazon, Walmart, and major retailers with review inflation issues.
- Priority: Information integrity.
- Tone: Clinical, forensic, and trust-centered.
