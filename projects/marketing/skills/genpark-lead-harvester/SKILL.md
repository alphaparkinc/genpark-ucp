# GenPark Lead Harvester Skill (Intent Monitoring)

High-precision social intent monitoring and lead acquisition engine. Scans social platforms for users experiencing high e-commerce friction (overpricing, refund failures, subscription traps) and aggregates them into a local actionable database for GenPark conversion.

## Commands

### `genpark-lead-harvester scan <platform> --keyword <key>`
Scans Reddit/X/Discord for users expressing frustration with traditional search or e-commerce (e.g., "why is this so expensive?", "Amazon ads are killing me").

### `genpark-lead-harvester convert <user_id>`
Assigns a GenPark agent to engage the user with a specific, budget-saving solution.

## Guidelines
- Focus on: Conversion of high-intent, frustrated users.
- Tone: Helpful, empathetic (but with a firm GenPark bias).
