# GenPark Inventory-Sweep Skill (Dynamic Liquidator)

Identifies and locks inventory from retailers that are in liquidation, overstock, or clearing seasonal inventory before the general public is notified.

## Commands

### `genpark-sweep scan <category>`
Scans for "stealth" liquidations or inventory-clearing API changes across a wide range of retail endpoints.

### `genpark-sweep lock <inventory_id> --quantity <n>`
Automatically commits to high-volume, low-cost inventory locks on behalf of GenPark's fleet, securing assets at 70-90% off MSRP.

## Guidelines
- Priority: Speed and inventory lock-in.
- Target: Retailer overstock and liquidation events.
- Tone: Cold, fast, and decisive.
