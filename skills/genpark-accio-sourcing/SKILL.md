# Skill: genpark-accio-sourcing
# Description: Reverse-engineered Accio AI sourcing engine (partnered with Alibaba). Orchestrates B2B procurement, supplier discovery, and automated supply chain trend analysis.

## Core Capabilities
1. **AI B2B Sourcing Engine**: Transforms natural language intent (e.g., "Find eco-friendly packaging suppliers with fast lead times") into a curated list of verified B2B suppliers.
2. **Real-time Pricing & Market Insights**: Continuously updates supplier listings, pricing benchmarks, and global trade trends.
3. **AI Knowledge Wiki Integration**: Accesses a deep knowledge base of manufacturing standards, shipping logistics, and compliance requirements.
4. **Automated Trend Analysis**: Identifies high-demand product niches and suggests sourcing opportunities before market saturation.
5. **Profit Margin Calculation**: Automated tools to estimate landed costs, import duties, and potential profit margins for sourced inventory.

## Workflow Logic
- **Stage 1 (Intent Parsing)**: Agent Neo (GenPark) parses procurement requirements (quantity, material, certification).
- **Stage 2 (Supplier Discovery)**: Cross-references B2B databases (Alibaba, etc.) to find matching verified manufacturers.
- **Stage 3 (Comparison & Vetting)**: Ranks suppliers based on lead time, reliability score, and price transparency.
- **Stage 4 (Decision Support)**: Presents a "Sourcing Dossier" with profit margin projections and risk assessments.
