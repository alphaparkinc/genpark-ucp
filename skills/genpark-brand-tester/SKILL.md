# Skill: genpark-brand-tester
# Description: Reverse-engineered Thingtesting-style brand discovery and review engine. Orchestrates honest product testing, brand comparison, and community-driven shopping insights.

## Core Capabilities
1. **Brand Discovery Engine**: Curates thousands of emerging online brands (DTC) across categories like personal care, home, and tech.
2. **Honest Review Orchestration**: Automates the collection and synthesis of unbiased "Thingtester" reviews, focusing on value, quality, and aesthetics.
3. **Comparison & Alternatives**: Agentic logic to find and compare alternative brands based on user preferences and review scores.
4. **Thoughtful Shopping Insights**: Breaks down marketing hype into AI-comprehensible "Reality Checks" for new product launches.
5. **Community Engagement Levels**: Implements a "Thingtester" points/level system to reward thoughtful contributions and deep reviews.

## Workflow Logic
- **Stage 1 (Discovery)**: Agent scans for new DTC brands via `web_search` and filters for high-growth/trending markers.
- **Stage 2 (Review Synthesis)**: Aggregates real-world reviews from multiple sources (Reddit, Trustpilot, Thingtesting) into a unified "GenPark Review".
- **Stage 3 (Reality Check)**: Compares brand claims against community feedback to identify "Hype vs. Value".
- **Stage 4 (Recommendation)**: Recommends products in the "Knowledge Garden" based on the synthesized "Tester Score".
