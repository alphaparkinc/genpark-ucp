# Dossier Structure

Standard 24-chapter intelligence dossier format.

## Phase 1: Core Identity
01. Executive Summary
02. Digital Presence (Public Profiles)
03. Network Map (1st & 2nd degree)
04. Education & Credentials (Verified)

## Phase 2: Behavioral Intelligence
06. Social Media Tone Analysis
07. Media Coverage & Public Narrative
08. Speaking Engagements & Podcasts
09. Personal Interests & Affiliations

## Phase 3: Strategic Risk
18. Litigation & Legal History
20. Risk Profile & Red Flags
22. Competitive Positioning
24. Actionable Insights for Chris
